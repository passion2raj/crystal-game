How to play Game - Game Rule

  1. Each new game, You will get TargetScore randomly.
  2. Each crystal will generate random number, when you click on it.
  3. All those number store in Your score (addition).
  4. If your score match to Target Score , You will win! & add to win-count.
  5. If your score increse than Target Score , You will loose & add to loss-count
